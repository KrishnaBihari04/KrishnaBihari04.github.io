About this portfolio

I created this portfolio as part of a school assignment during P4, the final term of my first year. I built this website completely from scratch, without using any templates, examples, or inspiration from other websites. I even made the color scheme myself. Everything you see here is the result of my own creativity and ideas. Because of that, I don’t have any screenshots or references to inspiration sites—this project is 100% original. The goal of the assignment was to create a portfolio to showcase what I’ve learned so far, including my projects, skills, and how I’ve grown throughout the year.

There’s a section on this site where I keep my most up-to-date projects. It includes short descriptions, the tools I used, and sometimes links to the code or working versions. I try to keep it updated to reflect what I’m currently working on or learning about. Feel free to look around and see what I’ve done and how I’m improving as I go!
